The Intelligence BehindDrone Atlas

A deep-learning platform built to identify, classify, and catalogue unmanned aerial vehicles — making UAV analysis accessible to operators, researchers, and security professionals worldwide.

// 01 — MISSION

Why We Built This

The rapid proliferation of UAV technology has created an urgent need for fast, accurate drone identification. Whether for airspace management, security operations, or academic research — knowing what you're looking at matters.

Drone Atlas was built to democratize that capability. We fine-tuned MobileNetV2 on a curated dataset of 2,251 drone images across five distinct archetypes, achieving classification accuracy that rivals specialist hardware — delivered through a browser interface in seconds.

Our system is designed to evolve: every classified scan refines the model's understanding, and our Atlas database grows with every operator who joins the network.
